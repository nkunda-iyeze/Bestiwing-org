Who Are We?

BEST WING ORGANIZATION IS A HUMANITARIAN ADVOCACY, DEVELOPMENTAL LOCAL NON GOVERNMENTAL ORGANIZATION THAT FOCUSSES ON THE WELL BEING OF THE CHILDREN, THEIR FAMILIES AND THE COMMUNITIES.

The Organization’s name “Best Wing Organization” is taken from book of psalms 91:4, He will cover you with his feathers, and under his wings you will find refuge, his faithfulness will be your shield and rampart.

Learn More

GENERAL OBJECTIVE

General objective of BWO is to promote sustainable development in the community through promotion of health, education, child protection, gender and social inclusion, and socio economic development through activities as training, advocacy, competition, community dialogues and campaigns..

Learn More

BWO VISION STATEMENT

To value dignity and uniqueness of hopeless children within the community we serve and envision to build the community and family in any aspect of life for self-reliance.

Learn More

BWO MISSION STATEMENT

Promote the development and survival of the most vulnerable children by observing their basic human rights and help them and their families to be self-reliant.
